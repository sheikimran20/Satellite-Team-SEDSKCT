About Us


Mission

To develop satellites for space exploration by researching, innovating, and building them, while enabling the Satellite Team with the abilities to develop through various phases of design, analysis, fabrication, testing, and launch.


Vision

To establish research and learning environment for students interested in acquiring knowledge and experience in satellite and space technology. And also to encourage students to pursue careers in the space industry.
